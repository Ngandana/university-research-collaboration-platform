# Stakeholder Analysis

## Overview

This section identifies key stakeholders involved in the University Research Collaboration Platform and outlines their roles, concerns, pain points, and success metrics.

---

## Stakeholder Analysis Table

| Stakeholder           | Role                                              | Key Concerns                                                          | Pain Points                                                       | Success Metrics                         |
| --------------------- | ------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------- |
| Students              | Conduct research and collaborate with supervisors | Easy access to research materials, clear task tracking, communication | Scattered tools (email, WhatsApp, Google Drive), missed deadlines | 30% improvement in task completion rate |
| Supervisors           | Guide and monitor student research                | Visibility of student progress, efficient feedback mechanisms         | Difficulty tracking multiple students, delayed submissions        | 40% faster feedback turnaround          |
| Researchers           | Collaborate on academic projects                  | Efficient document sharing, version control, collaboration tools      | Version conflicts, lack of centralized system                     | Reduced document duplication by 50%     |
| System Administrators | Manage system and users                           | System reliability, security, user management                         | Manual user management, system downtime                           | 99.9% system uptime                     |
| University Management | Oversee research output and performance           | Reporting, analytics, research productivity                           | Lack of centralized reporting tools                               | Improved reporting efficiency by 50%    |
| IT Support Staff      | Maintain system infrastructure                    | System stability, maintainability, scalability                        | Complex troubleshooting, poor documentation                       | Reduced incident resolution time by 40% |
